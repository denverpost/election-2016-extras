2016 active registered voter turnout

As of the morning of Nov. 9, 79.8 percent (2,610,897) of active voters had voted. The percentage of ballots returned is based on 3,269,918 ballots mailed out to active voters as of Nov. 2.

2016 all registered voter turnout

Among all registered voters, both active and inactive, the turnout as of Nov. 8 is 68 percent. Colorado's final voter turnout for the 2012 presidential election was 71 percent.
